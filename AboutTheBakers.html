Meet The Bakers!

N.D. Bakery is ran by two main bakers, Noor and Dalal. Take some time to learn about them here!

Dalal

Hi! My name is Dalal and I am one of the 2 bakers for N.D. bakery. I am 17 years old and go to school at the Early College at Guilford. I enjoy baking, playing soccer, hanging out with friends, and reading in my free time. I love baking all types of things, but I specialize in cakes and cupcakes. I love cake decorating and bringing my desserts to life, so I am in charge of the custom orders! My favorite dessert is Eclairs! Thank you for taking the time to visit our site!

Noor

Hello! My name is Noor and I am the second baker for N.D. Bakery. I am 15 years old and go to school at Northern Guilford. I enjoy baking, running, watching movies, and hanging out with my friends. I also like playing volleyball. I specialize in making the non-cake desserts, like pies, muffins, and cookies! My favorite dessert is strawbeery shortcake.Thank you for checking out our bakery!
